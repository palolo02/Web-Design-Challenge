1. Gun Data (csv)

"Gun Violence Archive (GVA) is a not for profit corporation formed in 2013 to provide free online public access to accurate information about gun-related violence in the United States. GVA will collect and check for accuracy, comprehensive information about gun-related violence in the U.S. and then post and disseminate it online." - Kaggle

2. Pittsburgh (csv)

Data was collected from Public PA State US and Data gov US Sites (this data is public and can be used for different purposes). In order to have a significant data set to work with, we choose a Crime data set from 2005 to 2015. Education and Income data is not available the same period, so we have only 2 years 2014 and 2015. We believed that it will get us enough data to perform our analysis.
